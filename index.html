Competitive Rules

Battle Clauses

  • Species Clause: One Pokemon per species on your team
  • Sleep Clause: Only one opponent Pokemon can be asleep at a time
  • OHKO Clause: One-hit KO moves are banned
  • Evasion Clause: Double Team and Minimize are banned

Tournament Rules

  • Item Clause: No duplicate items in tournaments
  • All Pokemon must be level 100 for gym battles
  • 6v6 singles format for gym challenges
  • Best-of-3 matches for tournament elimination rounds
